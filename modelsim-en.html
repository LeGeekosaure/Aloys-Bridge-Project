Rules

Scale

Before starting the construction of a model, it's important to determine its scale so that the size of an element on the model can be measured. We decided to use a scale of 1/200, meaning that a human figure is about 10mm tall.

Creating Characters

Modeling the characters

Creating and editing 3D models is essential for crafting small-scale characters. I used software like MakeHuman and Blender to create and adapt human models and clothing based on our source of inspiration.

Printing

Resin

Resin 3D printing is the best solution we've found to achieve so many details at such a small size. That's why we chose it.

Support Removal

The pure hell

Support removal is a crucial step, although we'd gladly skip it. It's the step where we need to remove all the supports from a part to restore its original shape.

For shelter

Building houses for our characters is a necessity. Thanks to the girls for their amazing work.
